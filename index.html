Key Lessons

8 lessons that change how you pitch

ピッチの常識を変える重要なポイント

01

Clarity Beats Complexity

If you can’t explain your solution in plain language, investors assume you don’t fully understand it yourself. Kill the jargon.

02

Pitching Is a Performance

It’s not a report or a presentation — it’s a show. Use voice, body language, and emotional rhythm to connect with your audience.

03

The 10-Slide Framework

Intro, Problem, Value Proposition, Solution, Business Model, Competition, Team, Global Strategy, CTA, and Conclusion — in that order.

04

Q&A Is Your Secret Weapon

Prepare 5–10 tough questions in advance. If handled well, Q&A can elevate your entire pitch and build deep credibility.

05

Storytelling Wins Deals

The 3 essential elements: Why you started, the Struggle you faced, and the Happy Ending you’re building. Emotion creates memory.

06

The Multiverse Approach

Prepare 3 versions: 5-second hook, 30-second teaser, and 5-minute full story. Start short, earn the longer conversation.

07

One Voice, One Message

Pick strategy over fairness. One confident, globally experienced speaker is stronger than three nervous ones splitting slides.

08

Insert a “Wow” Moment

A surprising stat, bold claim, or emotional story in the first 30 seconds breaks the pattern and locks in attention.
